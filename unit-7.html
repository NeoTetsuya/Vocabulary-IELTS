2. Tourism Statistics

Target: 100%

When talking about statistics we say that figures reach a peak of or peak at:
The number of visitors reached a peak of 10,000.

The opposite of a peak is a low. The word trough also has this meaning, but is usually used together with the word peak:
The tourism industry has its peaks and troughs.

3. Travel Errors

  • Travel / Travelling = moving from place to place.
  • Travel = a verb and an uncountable noun.
  • Trip = short holiday or time away.
  • Journey = getting from A to B.
  • Luggage is uncountable (all of your bags).
