Grammar & Prepositions

Target: 100%

Vocabulary Note

Note the way we use prepositions with numbers and dates:

  • In 1997 the number fell to 10%
  • In 1997 the number fell by 25%
  • In 1997 the number fell from 35%

NOT in 35%; The number dropped to 10% between 1995 and 1997. NOT reduced to; By 1997 the number had fallen to 10%.

2. Diagrams & Processes

Target: 100%

Test Tip

When you are describing a process you need to organise the steps logically. To help signal this, you should use words such as: First, then, next, after this, finally.

If you use the conjunction when, be careful to use the simple present tense: The machine switches off when the bag is full. NOT when the bag will be full.
