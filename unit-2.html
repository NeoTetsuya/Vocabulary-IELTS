The final stage before adulthood is adolescencethe stage between childhood and adulthood 🔊. This is a period of transitiona change from one state to another 🔊 for teenagers and there are many crucial milestones. Socially and emotionally, teens worry that they may not be developing at the same rate as their peerspeople of the same age or status 🔊. They become extremely self-conscious and may be overly sensitive about their appearance. Teens may rebel against their parents but are also more able to accept the consequencesresults or effects of an action 🔊 of their actions.

This is also a period of enormous physical change and adolescents experience changes in their physical development at a rate unparalleledhaving no equal; exceptional 🔊 since infancy. These changes include significant gains in height and weight. Within a year, boys and girls can gain an average of 4.1 inches and 3.5 inches in height respectively. This growth spurt typically occurs two years earlier for girls than for boys and can tend to make both sexes go through a clumsy phase. In terms of their cognitiveconnected with thinking or mental processes 🔊 development, adolescents have greater reasoning skills and have developed the ability to think logically and hypothetically. They are also able to discuss more abstractexisting in thought, not physical 🔊 conceptsideas or principles 🔊. They should also have developed strategies to help them study.
